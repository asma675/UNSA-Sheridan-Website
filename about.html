About UNSA Sheridan

We’re a student-led club at Sheridan College focused on global issues, the United Nations, and creating meaningful action through events, workshops, and campus collaboration.

What we believe

CuriosityAsk better questions about the world.
CommunityMeet people who care and want to contribute.
ImpactTurn learning into action with projects and volunteering.

What we do

Examples you can customize:

WorkshopsDiplomacy, public speaking, policy writing, Model UN skills.
EventsGuest speakers, SDG panels, cultural nights, awareness drives.
ChallengesSDG Innovation Challenge — teams build solutions and pitch.

Who can join?

Everyone. If you’re interested in global affairs, humanitarian work, sustainable development, or just want to meet people and grow your leadership skills — you belong here.
